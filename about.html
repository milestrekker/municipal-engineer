Department Overview

The Office of the Municipal Engineer is responsible for planning, implementing, and maintaining infrastructure projects within the Municipality of Isulan. Our team works together to ensure the safety, efficiency, and sustainability of our community's infrastructure.

Mission and Vision

Mission

To provide efficient and effective engineering services through proper planning, implementation, and maintenance of infrastructure projects while ensuring public safety and sustainable development.

Vision

To be a leading engineering office that delivers quality infrastructure services, promotes sustainable development, and ensures the safety and well-being of the community.
